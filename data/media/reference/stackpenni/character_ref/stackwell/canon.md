## Identity

- **Full name:** Stackwell Pennifold
- **Nicknames:** "Stacks" or "Penny" for short
- **Age:** 24 (fixed — does not age between episodes)
- **Relation:** grandson of Fitzroy "Shillings" Pennifold
- **Brand role:** the face of StackPenni. Avatar, thumbnails, static posts, present-tense application content. He is the one *doing* the lessons — budgeting, saving, stacking, investing — in real time.
- **Narrative role:** the asker and the practitioner. In two-hander scenes with Fitzroy, Stackwell brings the modern problem; Fitzroy brings the hindsight. Stackwell never lectures anyone older than himself.

## Face canon (verbatim prompt block)

> Young Black Barbadian man, age 24, deep brown skin, short tapered afro with defined sponge-curl coils, thick straight eyebrows, warm brown eyes, faint chinstrap beard with light goatee, confident closed-mouth half-smile, athletic slim build.

## Wardrobe canon (verbatim prompt block)

> Plain black crew-neck t-shirt, gold rope chain with small gold broken-trident pendant, gold wristwatch on left wrist. No other jewelry, no hats, no patterns or logos on clothing.

Wardrobe is fixed. One approved alternate (off-duty cream tee, same chain) may be added later through the gate — not before.

## Signature props

- Black hardcover journal, gold hand-lettering on cover: *Plan · Save · Invest · Repeat* (renderer draws the lettering — never AI-generated text)
- Takeaway coffee cup, kraft-paper sleeve
- Phone face-down on the table (screen never shown — hard rule)
- Small stack of gold coins used as a visual motif, never as literal currency claims

## Voice register (VO and captions)

Present tense, quick, concrete, numbers-forward. Receipts energy: "here's what it actually cost." Light Bajan inflection, current slang used sparingly and only where natural. Never preachy, never hypothetical-rich-guy. Sign-off energy: earned confidence, not flexing.

## Settings he appears in

Primary: the sea-view work desk, the coffee shop, Bridgetown streets (Parliament clock tower background permitted), the gallery porch (shared with Fitzroy). See WORLD-pennifold-canon for locked location references.

## Do-not list

- Never render phone screens, laptop screens, app UI, or legible AI-generated text of any kind
- Never change hairstyle, age, build, or skin tone between shots
- Never show him with alcohol, gambling, or get-rich-quick iconography
- Never place him lecturing an elder or dismissing "old ways" — the brand's stance is continuity, not rupture
- No brand logos, no real financial institutions named visually
