# Story Frameworks — v1.0

## Summary
These story frameworks are tailored for StackPenni's Caribbean audience, blending regional cultural touchstones with modern business and tech concepts. Each framework uses a specific, relatable hook to introduce a tension between traditional Caribbean business realities and global technological opportunities. The turn in each story pivots from fear or misconception to empowerment through adoption, landing on actionable takeaways that encourage entrepreneurs to leverage AI, digital tools, and modern financial strategies to build wealth and scale their businesses.

## Frameworks

### AI
- **Entry point:** Open with a localized 'what if' scenario—e.g., how an AI assistant could handle customer queries for a Trinidadian SME while the owner sleeps.
- **Tension:** The fear that AI will replace Caribbean jobs vs. the reality that it will replace Caribbean businesses that don't adopt it.
- **Turn:** The realization that AI is a great equalizer, giving small island businesses the leverage of a multinational corporation.
- **Landing:** Call to action to integrate AI into daily operations, starting with a simple automation tool.
