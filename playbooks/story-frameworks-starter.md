# Playbook: Story Frameworks Starter

*Repo location: `playbooks/story-frameworks-starter.md` · Executed by the system's AI during onboarding, through the console. v1.0*

## Purpose

How to tell a story per subject type for this business (e.g., money lesson, tech explainer, cultural observation).

## Inputs

Subject taxonomy (from Business Profile) + admired examples (from Viral Patterns intake) + 2–3 stories the user tells often (spoken, from the Voice interview or seed voice notes).

## Procedure

1. For each core subject type, AI drafts a framework: entry point, tension, turn, landing — grounded in one admired example and one of the user's own told stories.
2. Frameworks must be voice-compatible (checked against Voice Profile).
3. Gate → v1.

## Output

Story Frameworks module: one compact framework per subject type, each with a real example.

## Gate

User picks/rejects per framework.
